About Me

I'm Hari, a data scientist with an MS in Data Science from the University of Washington. I work at the intersection of statistics, machine learning, and data engineering — building systems that turn complex datasets into decisions.

Most recently I built predictive ML models for surgical decision-making at Virginia Mason Hospital, working directly with surgeons to bridge the gap between data science and real-world clinical impact. Before that I spent two years at US Bank as an Analyst, building production SQL pipelines, ETL workflows, and dashboards that drove operational decisions at scale.
